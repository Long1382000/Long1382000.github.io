This is the result of an online test to help me better understand myself and assist me in future job orientation. The test applies the style of answering multiple choice questions so that you can statistically and analyze your strengths and weaknesses and give the most satisfactory results about career orientation through questions. The result of this test is quite similar to the things that I have oriented myself and always try to complete it. Sometimes there are other options with my personal opinion, but maybe I'll use them to try to improve myself.

According to the result of the Myers-Briggs test, I can recognize my strengths and use them to improve myself. In general, I am an extrovert person, because I always want to explore new things and helps others. On the other hand, I am also an intuitive person, which helps me to predict different situations can happen in my job or life. Besides, my feeling takes about 60% in my mind, which shows that I am an emotional person. I always take care of and concern about both human and animals.

However, it is also my weakness in my job or relationship when I spend a lot of time to pay attention to other thinking or emotion. The feeling makes me be hard to make a decision by myself. In addition, I am a judging person, and I love the right. I usually consider everything in different aspects and concentrate on the positive sides. This helps me to overcome the difficulties in my life. My turbulent sides account for 56%. It is a big number, which makes me always lose control of everything around me. After that, I can not know what to do next. Therefore, this test gives me an exact result, which makes me understand more about myself and know how to change to get better day by day. Moreover, it also helps me to choose the best job in the future, which be suitable for the ability of myself.
